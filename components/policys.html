Payment Policy

We aim to make the ordering process for your customizable photo frames as smooth as possible. Please read the following terms carefully before placing an order:

Accepted Payment Methods

We currently accept payments via UPI methods, including Google Pay and PhonePe. Details for payment will be shared during the order process on WhatsApp.

Payment Requirements

To confirm and start processing your order, full payment is required. Alternatively, you may choose to pay 50% of the total amount upfront to receive a design preview. If you approve the design, the remaining 50% must be paid to proceed with production and dispatch.

Design Approval and Cancellations

If you decide to cancel the order after receiving the design preview, we will refund 50% of the upfront payment. Once the design is approved and the remaining payment is made, the order will be processed, and no cancellations or refunds will be allowed.

No Refunds for Custom Orders

As all our products are customized to your specifications, refunds will not be issued after the final payment and once production has started.

Payment Confirmation

Please ensure you share proof of payment (such as a screenshot) with us on WhatsApp for faster processing of your order.

By placing an order, you agree to the terms outlined in this payment policy. For any questions or concerns, feel free to contact us on WhatsApp.

Return/Refund Policy

Thank you for choosing our customizable photo frames. Please note that as our products are specially made to your specifications, the following terms apply:

No Returns on Customized Products

All our products are custom-made based on your preferences and approval. Once the product is created and dispatched, returns are not accepted unless the product is damaged during delivery.

Damage During Delivery

If your product arrives damaged, please contact us within 48 hours of receiving it. To process your claim, provide the following:

  • Clear photos of the damaged product.
  • Photos of the packaging showing the damage.

Upon verification, we will offer a replacement or refund based on the extent of the damage.

Order Cancellations

Cancellations are only allowed before the final approval of the design. Please refer to our Payment Policy for details on refunds for cancelled orders after design previews.

No Refunds for Approved Designs

Once you approve the design and the remaining payment is made, production begins. At this stage, refunds are not possible.

Customer Responsibility

Ensure that all customization details (e.g., photos, text, or special requests) are accurate when placing the order. We are not liable for errors in submitted details.

Contact Us

For any issues or concerns regarding your order, please reach out to us on WhatsApp. Our team will assist you promptly.
